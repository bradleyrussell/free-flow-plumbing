Comprehensive Plumbing & Underground Services

Free‑Flow Plumbing provides a full suite of plumbing and utility services for homeowners, contractors and property managers throughout Pasco and Pinellas counties. Below you’ll find detailed descriptions of our core offerings. If you don’t see what you need, please call — we likely do that too!

Sewer & Drain Services

Repeated clogs and backups may signal a failing main sewer line. Free‑Flow Plumbing specialises in diagnosing and replacing failing sewer pipes — from patch repairs to complete replacements. We employ camera inspections to locate the problem, provide a clear explanation and work meticulously to protect your landscaping.

  • Full sewer line replacement & spot repairs
  • Drain cleaning & hydro‑jetting
  • Camera inspections & leak detection
  • Trenchless options where feasible
  • Storm drain installation & repair

Replacing a sewer line can be disruptive — but it doesn’t have to be. We own compact excavation equipment that minimises yard impact and we restore your property upon completion. Our experience with permitting ensures the job moves quickly.

New Construction & Additions

Building a new home, adding a second story or converting a garage? Proper plumbing rough‑ins are critical for long‑term performance. We collaborate with homeowners and builders to design and install code‑compliant systems that integrate seamlessly into the building envelope.

  • Complete plumbing rough‑ins for additions, ADUs & new builds
  • Bathroom & kitchen remodel plumbing
  • Garage & pool house conversions with full plumbing
  • Coordination with general contractors & inspectors

From foundation trenches to final fixtures, Free‑Flow Plumbing ensures your project stays on schedule and meets all local codes. Our owner‑operated approach means you have a single point of contact throughout construction.

Excavation & Drainage

Water management is critical in Florida’s climate. Whether you need a storm drain for a gated community or trenching for utility lines, our compact excavators and experienced operators deliver clean, controlled digging and proper drainage solutions.

  • Storm water drain installation & French drains
  • Site trenching for utilities & irrigation
  • Grading & land shaping for proper runoff
  • Repair & replacement of collapsed culverts

We protect surrounding structures and landscaping while delivering long‑lasting drainage solutions. Ask about integrating drainage into your next improvement or solving persistent flooding issues at your property’s entrance.

Get a Drainage Assessment

Repairs & Water Heaters

No job is too small. From drippy faucets to failing water heaters, we provide the same level of care and expertise on repairs as we do on large projects. We also specialise in installing high‑efficiency water heaters that deliver endless hot water while saving energy.

  • Leak repair & pipe replacement
  • Fixture installations (toilets, faucets, sinks, showers, tubs)
  • Repipes & whole‑home upgrades
  • Water heater repair & replacement (tank & tankless)
  • Water softener & filtration system installation

We stock common parts on our truck to fix most problems on the first visit and we supply high‑quality fixtures and heaters through trusted distributors. Our goal is to get your home up and running quickly with craftsmanship that lasts.
