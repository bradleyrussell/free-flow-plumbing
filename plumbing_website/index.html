Why Sewer Lines Fail (And Why You Need an Expert)

Your sewer line is buried underground, so you don't think about it‚Äîuntil it fails. Tree roots, ground shifting, old cast iron pipes, and Florida's unique soil conditions can all cause sewer lines to crack, collapse, or clog. When that happens, you need someone with excavation equipment, experience, and the skills to do it right.

Warning Signs You Need Sewer Line Service

  • Frequent backups or slow drains throughout the house
  • Gurgling sounds from toilets or drains
  • Sewage odors in your yard or home
  • Patches of unusually green or soggy grass
  • Multiple plumbing fixtures backing up at once
  • Foundation cracks or unexplained settling
