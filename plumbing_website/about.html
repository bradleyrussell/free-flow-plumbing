Our Story

Free‑Flow Plumbing, Inc. is the vision of master plumber Scott Russell. After honing his craft in the Tampa Bay area, Scott founded Free‑Flow Plumbing in 2008 with a simple mission: deliver reliable plumbing services at an honest price, treat every home like it’s his own and stay personally involved in every job.

Over 16 years later, that mission remains the same. Scott still answers the phone, writes the estimates and climbs into the trenches. When you work with Free‑Flow Plumbing you aren’t hiring a faceless chain — you’re working directly with a local expert who stakes his reputation on the quality of your project.

We’ve earned an A+ rating with the Better Business Bureau and hundreds of five‑star reviews because our customers appreciate the difference that personal accountability makes. From small repairs to complete sewer replacements and new construction rough‑ins, we show up on time, work cleanly and respect your property.
